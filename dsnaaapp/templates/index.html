Our Objectives

Our Mission is to empower people and governments to embrace Artificial Intelligence so they can impact their communities and promote a Data-Driven economies (vision) through a humain and trustworthy technologies (Values)

Helping North African countries and the Arab world to build AI strategies.

Building the capacity of researchers, students and youth in data science issues

Encouraging cooperation between industry, universities, and scientific research sectors.

Promoting research activities related to data science in North African countries and the Arab world.

Foster the promotion of AI ecosystem in North Africa and Arab countries.

Raising awareness about data science among governments, women and youth.

Supporting the implementation of Ai “uses-cases” in both public and private sector

Organizing national and international events, conferences and workshops related to data science.
